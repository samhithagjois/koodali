Namma Kannada Shaale Homework leaderboard

This is the homework leaderboard where teachers and admins can see which kid did their homework the best.

If you are a teacher, you can edit this leaderboard by a) uploading your excel to your page b) entering the scores manually by clicking on each student on your student overview page. You cannot edit a student who is not in one of the sections that you teach.

Admins can edit the leaderboard by adding and removing students.

The leaderboard can be filtered by the following : 1. advanced/intermediate/beginner 2. country 3. highest to lowest 4. lowest to highest
